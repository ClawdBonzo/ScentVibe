## NOTES ON DATA QUALITY

- All fragrances listed are REAL products currently sold in the US/Western market
- Brand names and fragrance names are exact commercial names
- Gender classifications follow industry standard marketing (M=masculine, F=feminine, U=unisex)
- Price tiers: Budget (under $50 for typical size), Mid ($50-150), Luxury ($150+)
- Amazon bestseller rankings fluctuate; these represent consistent top performers 2023-2025
- Sephora bestsellers overlap heavily with department store rankings
- Niche/unisex category weighted toward Fragrantica community favorites
- Middle Eastern-origin budget brands (Lattafa, Armaf, Rasasi, Afnan, Al Haramain) have surged in US popularity via Amazon 2023-2025
- Celebrity fragrances (Ariana Grande especially) dominate budget women's category
- Tom Ford Private Blend line straddles unisex/niche categories
- Maison Margiela Replica line is a major Sephora performer
