Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

ScentMatch AI ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the ScentMatch AI mobile application.

Information We Collect

Photos you scan: When you scan a photo, it is analyzed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. Photos are not uploaded to any server. Compressed thumbnail images are stored locally on your device in SwiftData for your match history.

Match history: Your scan results, vibe scores, and fragrance recommendations are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

Analytics events: We log basic usage events (scans completed, recommendations tapped, affiliate links tapped) locally on your device in SwiftData. These are not transmitted to any server. You can export or delete this data at any time from Settings.

Subscription data: If you subscribe to ScentMatch Pro, your subscription is managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not upload your photos to any server
  • We do not use tracking pixels, advertising SDKs, or analytics services
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use cookies (the app has no web component)
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes

On-Device Processing

All image analysis, fragrance matching, and recommendation generation happens entirely on your device. The Vision framework color extraction, mood analysis, and matching engine all run locally. The fragrance database of 510+ scents is bundled with the app. No internet connection is required for core functionality.

Affiliate Links

When you tap "Shop on Amazon" for a fragrance, you are redirected to Amazon.com (or your regional Amazon store) through an Amazon Associates affiliate link. Amazon's own privacy policy governs your interaction with their site. We earn a commission on qualifying purchases but do not receive any personal information about your Amazon activity.

Data Storage and Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. You can delete all match history from Settings at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

Children's Privacy

ScentMatch AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@scentmatch.app.
